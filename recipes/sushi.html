California rolls

a Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, often topped with or rolled around raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, or eggs, and is known for its variety of styles and flavors.

Ingridients

  • rice
  • fish
  • nori
  • wasabi

steps

  1. Rinse sushi rice until the water runs clear, then cook and season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, shiny side down.
  3. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
